16 April at 01:38

I heard about a study recently, and I'd love to hear more about it.

For years now there's been this conversation about how people are ruder and bolder online, that the anonymity of the internet means people are meaner than theyd ever be in real life.

The study concluded this isnt true AT ALL.

That actually, if you're an asshole on the internet, youre also an asshole in real life, and vice versa.

Polite and quiet and kind people are everywhere, and they tend to be quiet on the internet too, making most conversations on the web Troll vs Troll.

Asshole vs Asshole.

.

Your thoughts welcomed.

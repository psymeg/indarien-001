Now, where was I? Oh, i figured out linking progress to social media doesn’t work because i dont always feel like it, so i made a handy chart in a book, and added colour as an executive function tip. Anyway, for many days i forgot i made this, which would have made some of these numbers higher, but not all. I think my longest walk this year was about 15km. And i've read a lot this year. But man, i really dont like typing poems. And i don't dance unless i do it on purpose. Ideally it's more about doing things regularly, of course, this is just for fun. I've dropped some things off the list, and I welcome suggestions for new categories.

For those of you playing at home, i'm up to:

  • 10 yoga stretches
  • 9 papers filed
  • 9 pages read
  • 7 friends talked with
  • 7 km walked
  • 6 minutes danced
  • 2 poems typed
